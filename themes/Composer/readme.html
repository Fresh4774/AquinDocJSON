Composer

Last updated 10/5/2024

Overview

The composer in the Aquin app provides several functions to enhance your interaction with the AI, including options to upload files, use a microphone, and send messages. The composer acts as your main interface for sending inputs to the AI.

Functions in the Composer

  • Upload File: Attach files to share with the AI.
  • Send Button: Submit your text prompt to the AI.
  • Microphone Button: Send voice commands or messages to the AI.

The composer is located at the bottom of the app and acts as a large bar with interactive buttons for these functionalities.

How to Use the Composer

1. Sending Messages: Type your message into the composer and press the Send Button to submit it, or use the Return/Enter key on your keyboard for quick sending.

2. Using Voice Input: To send a message via voice, press the Microphone Button and begin speaking. Once you're finished, press the button again to submit your voice message.

3. Uploading Files: To upload a file, click the Upload (Folder) Button to browse and select a file from your computer, or drag and drop a file directly into the composer area.

Summary

The composer is your primary tool for interacting with the AI, whether you're sending text messages, voice commands, or uploading files for analysis. This feature streamlines communication, making it easy to manage inputs in one place.
