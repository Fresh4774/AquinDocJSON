How to Create and Use a Pre-Prompt

Last updated 10/5/2024

Overview

The pre-prompt feature allows you to customize specific configurations that guide your AI assistant's behavior. Pre-prompts ensure that the AI responds according to a predefined context, saving time and maintaining consistency across tasks and interactions.

Creating a New Pre-Prompt

To create a new pre-prompt, click the "Create Pre-Prompt" button. You can define a prompt that will be used as a basis for all subsequent conversations or interactions. This is especially useful for tasks requiring the AI to operate within specific guidelines.

Customizing the Pre-Prompt

Once created, you can customize your pre-prompt further by modifying advanced settings such as temperature or context length. These options allow for greater flexibility, making sure the AI fits your specific needs.

Summary

The pre-prompt feature is a powerful tool for optimizing AI responses by providing clear guidance, ensuring consistent and efficient interactions across various tasks.
