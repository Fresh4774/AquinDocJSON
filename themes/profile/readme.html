How to Use the Profile Feature

Last updated 10/5/2024

Overview

The profile feature allows you to manage essential settings such as your display name, profile image, and how the AI interacts with you.

Profile Settings

In the profile settings, you can update your display name and upload a profile image. You can also specify what personal details you'd like the AI to use in order to provide better, more tailored responses. For example, providing preferences or context about your tasks helps the AI understand how to assist you better.

Resetting Your Account

If at any point you want to reset your account or delete your profile, you can log out and follow the instructions to reset your password or delete your account entirely. Please note that deleting your account is permanent, and this action cannot be undone.

Summary

The profile feature allows you to customize the AI experience by tailoring personal settings such as your name, image, and preferences, ensuring a personalized interaction with the AI.
