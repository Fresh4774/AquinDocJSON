Quick Settings

The Quick Settings feature allows you to access both presets and assistants simultaneously, simplifying navigation and enhancing efficiency. Quick Settings offer quick access to the most frequently used configurations, helping streamline workflows.

Using Quick Settings

You can use Quick Settings by clicking the dropdown on the sidebar. Here, you will see your presets and assistants, allowing you to switch between them quickly during interactions.

Customization

Quick Settings also allow customization of the interface by changing your preset configurations, setting default assistants, or making small adjustments to the system to fit your current needs.

Summary

Quick Settings enhance productivity by offering immediate access to key features, helping you navigate your workspace with ease and flexibility.
