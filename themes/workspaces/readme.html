Workspaces

Last updated 10/4/2024

Overview

A workspace is a way to organize and separate your chats, presets, prompts, files, collections, assistants, or tools. By creating separate workspaces, you can keep your tasks and projects neatly categorized and easily accessible without mixing information from different areas.

Creating and Switching Workspaces

To create a new workspace, click on the workspace name next to your profile icon in the sidebar and select "New Workspace." You can switch between different workspaces by clicking on their names in the workspace list.

Customizing Workspace Settings

Each workspace can be customized to suit your specific needs. You can change the workspace name, update the workspace image, and modify how the AI interacts with you within that workspace by adjusting its response settings.

Summary

Workspaces help you stay organized by allowing you to separate different tasks and projects, ensuring that your workflow remains streamlined and efficient.
