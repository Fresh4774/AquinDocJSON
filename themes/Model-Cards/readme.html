Model Cards

Last updated 10/5/2024

Overview

The model cards feature allows you to explore and select from various AI models available on the platform. This feature is located on the home page of the Aquin app, giving users quick access to a variety of models to interact with. Model cards provide a summary of each model’s strengths, features, and intended use cases.

Available Models

The Aquin app offers several AI models through the model cards interface. These include:

  • ChatGPT4o: A versatile conversational model designed for general purposes.
  • ChatGPT4: A more advanced version suitable for complex problem solving and conversations.
  • LLaMA: A lightweight model optimized for smaller tasks requiring less computational power.
  • Gemma: A custom model designed for specialized use cases, providing unique capabilities.

How to Use Model Cards

Each model card provides essential information about the model’s features and capabilities. To begin using a model, simply click on the card that corresponds to the AI you want to interact with. This will open a chat session with the selected model, allowing you to issue prompts and receive responses tailored to the model’s strengths.

Choosing the Right Model

Selecting the appropriate model depends on your task. For example, you may choose ChatGPT4 for in-depth discussions and problem solving, or LLaMA for quicker, simpler interactions that require fewer resources. The model cards make it easy to compare models and choose the best one for your current needs.

Summary

Model cards provide an efficient way to explore the available AI models on the platform. By offering clear summaries of each model’s strengths and capabilities, you can easily choose the one that best fits your specific requirements, ensuring a seamless interaction.
